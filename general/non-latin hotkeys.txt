If your system has a keyboard layout other than Latin,
it would be nice to use the vkNN key codes
in hotkey definitions for these keys:
  ; - vkBA
  , - vkBC
  . - vkBE
  / - vkBF
  [ - vkDB
  ] - vkDD
  ' - vkDE
  ` - vkC0

Example. Instead of this:
  ^`::MsgBox("Hello!")

Use this:
  ^vkC0::MsgBox("Hello!")


See the help for details:
https://www.autohotkey.com/docs/v2/Hotkeys.htm
